# Mollicute-Specific Primers

## Targets (Tier 1 — core panel)

| Target | Gene | Primer source | Rationale |
|--------|------|---------------|-----------|
| M. genitalium | MgPa adhesin | Jensen et al. | Independent HIV risk factor; 10% prevalence in SA |
| M. hominis | 16S rRNA (species-specific) | Multiple validated sets | 81% in HIV+ SA women; BV synergy; HIV shedding |
| U. parvum | ureC (species-specific) | Kong et al. 2000 | Dominant Ureaplasma; 77% in SA HIV+ women |
| U. urealyticum | ureC (species-specific) | Kong et al. 2000 | Less common but more pathogenic |

## Notes
- These are qPCR targets, not amplicon sequencing
- All four organisms lack cpn60 (Mollicute blind spot) and are underdetected by 16S universal primers
- FRESH already has M. genitalium presence/absence data (6.7% positive) — this panel adds M. hominis, Ureaplasma, and quantification
- All primers validated on vaginal swabs in published literature
- SA-specific prevalence data exists for all four targets

## Key references
- Kong et al. (2000). JCM, 38, 1175-1179 — Ureaplasma species-specific PCR
- Jensen et al. — MgPa qPCR (widely used clinical standard)
